# 泰拉转生模拟器 | Terra Reincarnation

An Arknights fan game where you reincarnate into the world of Terra and try to survive.

## Gameplay

1. **Allocate Stats** — Distribute 20 points across 5 attributes: Looks (颜值), Vitality (体质), Intelligence (智力), Luck (运气), and Wealth (家境)
2. **Choose Talents** — Pick 3 talents from a random pool. Talents range from Legendary (传说) and Rare (稀有) to negative (负面) traits
3. **Live Your Life** — Advance year by year through randomized events shaped by your race, nation, stats, and talents
4. **See Your Ending** — Die eventually (or not?) and read your epitaph

## Features

- 30+ playable races (Feline, Sarkaz, Sankta, Lung, Oni, Draco, and more)
- 18 nations with unique danger levels, wealth modifiers, and lore-accurate locations
- 50+ talents including legendary traits like 残存的记忆 (Doctor's Soul) and 不老体质 (Immortal Body)
- Background music from the Arknights OST
- Multiple endings and achievements

## Play

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No install or server required.

Live demo: [Vercel](https://terra-reborn.vercel.app) (may be inaccessible from China)
